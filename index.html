WHO WE ARE

NEW CLAIRE NHW

The objective of NHW is for neighbours to work together to prevent crime and therefore create a safe and friendly environment where crime is less likely to happen.

The main function of NHW is based on creating a local network of neighbours who help each other, keep an eye on each other, keep an eye on communal spaces, and keep an eye on their immediate environment.

By increasing the number of people working together to increase the level of supervision of homes and their environment — this is an effective means of combating burglary, theft and vandalism.

AIMS OF NHW

  • Increasing communication and knowledge amongst neighbours
  • Helping to prevent crime
  • Helping to develop community spirit
  • Providing a visible sign that residents care about their community
  • Encouraging reporting of suspicious activity
  • Improving personal and home security

BENEFITS OF NHW

  • Demonstrates community spirit
  • Prevents crime
  • Reduces fear of crime
  • Shows people care about their neighbourhood

CODE OF CONDUCT

This Code of Conduct is issued in terms of Regulation 4(2)(r) of the Western Cape Community Safety Act, 2013 (Act 3 of 2013). All members must pledge adherence.

01

Introduction

  1. A neighbourhood watch (NHW) must operate as the eyes and ears of the community it serves and must cooperate with SAPS.
  2. A NHW must assist SAPS and other safety services to safeguard the local community and their property.
  3. This Code of Conduct is binding on all members. Members must pledge adherence.
  4. By pledging adherence, a member acknowledges the risks involved in NHW activities.
02

Conduct of Members

  1. A member must at all times act in accordance with the Constitution of South Africa and uphold the aims of the NHW.
  2. A member may not discriminate unfairly or bring the NHW into disrepute.
  3. A member may not exploit membership for personal gain or accept unauthorised payment.
  4. Members must treat all community members with dignity and respect at all times.
03

Duties When Patrolling

  1. When patrolling, members must alert SAPS where possible and work in partnership with them.
  2. Members must carry identification cards and obey lawful instructions from police.
  3. Members must report suspicious activity to the control room and SAPS immediately.
  4. No vigilantism or intimidation is permitted under any circumstances.

Disciplinary Measures: Any breach of this Code of Conduct may result in the immediate termination of NHW membership. All members are expected to uphold these standards at all times to maintain the integrity of our neighbourhood watch.

WHATSAPP GROUP RULES

NEWCLAIR NHW GROUP

Our WhatsApp group is for residents of New Claire, Malmesbury ONLY. Stay on topic and help keep our community safe.


Contact us to be added once you are registered.

01You may not post content that infringes or violates someone else's rights or otherwise violates the law.
02You may not use the WhatsApp group to do anything unlawful, misleading, malicious, or discriminatory.
03You are solely responsible for your conduct and any content you submit. You may receive a warning or be removed.
04You may not abuse, harass, threaten, impersonate or intimidate any other users.
05You are responsible for any activity that occurs under your screen name.
06Always keep to the purpose of the group! Do not share irrelevant messages — this is a safety group, not a general chat.
07Post your message in one single chunk of text. Do not send multiple short messages in a row.
08Do not have one-on-one conversations in the group. Switch to private messages for personal discussions.
09If someone asks a question and you do not know the answer, just wait for someone who knows. Do not guess.
10Please do not send a hundred "thank you" messages. Use private messages for appreciation.
11Don't add random people — only for residents living within the New Claire Malmesbury area.
12Before posting, ask yourself: Is this relevant? Is this necessary? If unsure, don't post it.
